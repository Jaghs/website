The Last of Us

So, I've been a passionate fan of The Last of Us video game ever since I played the remastered version on PS4 a long time ago. To my excitement, during the summer of 2021, I discovered that HBO was shooting The Last of Us series in my hometown of Calgary!

Without hesitation, I applied to be on the show through local casting agencies, and my enthusiasm paid off when I received a call near the end of 2022. I was cast as a cannibal, a member of a small group of survivors led by David. This exciting opportunity led me to undergo multiple COVID-19 checks and participate in wardrobe sessions to perfect my character's appearance. However, a minor hurdle arose when the shooting dates coincided with my university commitments in February 2022. I had to seek permission from my professors for a temporary absence and even wrote a midterm in the hotel to make it all work üò™

I went to Waterton for the initial shooting amidst the stunning mountains. The experience was nothing short of surreal as I got to witness the cast and crew in action, offering a fascinating glimpse into the creation of big-budget media behind the scenes. Among the memorable encounters, I had the privilege to meet Ellie, brilliantly portrayed by Bella Ramsey, and even had the pleasure of meeting Troy Baker, the voice behind Joel in the game!
